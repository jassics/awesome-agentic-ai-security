# Contributing to Awesome Agentic AI Security

Thanks for helping build the best curated list for **Agentic AI**, **Agentic Engineering**, and **Agentic AI Security**! Contributions of all sizes are welcome.

## What belongs here

Resources that are **specific and useful** to agentic AI and its security, such as:

- Papers, standards, and threat taxonomies (OWASP, MITRE, NIST, ENISA, CSA, arXiv, etc.)
- Agent frameworks and agentic-engineering references
- Security tools, scanners, guardrails, and red-teaming tooling
- Books, videos, talks, courses, and certifications
- CTFs, labs, and intentionally vulnerable agent apps
- Real-world attacks, breaches, and incidents
- Governance, regulation, and compliance material

Please keep it **high quality and relevant** — this is a curated list, not a link dump.

## How to add a resource

1. Fork the repo and create a branch.
2. Add your link to the **most relevant section** in `README.md`.
3. Use this format, keeping entries alphabetical-ish where it helps:

   ```markdown
   - [Resource Title](https://link) — one concise sentence on what it is and why it's useful.
   ```

4. Prefer the primary/original source. Avoid affiliate or tracking links.
5. Check that your link works and isn't already listed.
6. Open a pull request describing what you added and why it's valuable.

## Style guidelines

- One resource per bullet, with a short em-dash description.
- Be neutral and factual; avoid marketing language.
- Keep to the existing section structure; propose a new section in your PR if needed.

## Reporting issues

Broken link, outdated resource, or a suggestion? Open an [issue](../../issues).
